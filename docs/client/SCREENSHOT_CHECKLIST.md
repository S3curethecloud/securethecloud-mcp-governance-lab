# Client Screenshot Checklist

Use this checklist when capturing final portfolio or client-share screenshots.

## Required Screenshots

1. Hero section with SecureTheCloud MCP Governance Lab title
2. Shared Trust Fabric cards
3. Public Demo Boundary / Client Story / Correct Claim cards
4. Executive Risk & Control Center
5. MCP Access Request Portal
6. MCP Tool-Call Firewall
7. Human Approval Workspace
8. Evidence Replay Timeline
9. Protected Demo Reset panel
10. Mock MCP Execution Adapter / approval-required execution result

## Screenshot Guidance

- Include the public Fly.io URL in at least one screenshot.
- Do not expose reset tokens.
- Do not expose terminal secrets.
- Do not show private browser tabs or account dashboards.
- Prefer full-width screenshots that show the polished product layout.
- Capture one screenshot at the top of the page and one screenshot near the evidence replay section.
